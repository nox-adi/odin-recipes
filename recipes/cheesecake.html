Cheesecake

Description

A cheesecake is a decadent dessert known for its rich and creamy texture. It typically consists of a firm, buttery crust made from crushed biscuits or graham crackers, which forms the base. The filling is a smooth mixture primarily made from cream cheese, eggs, sugar, and vanilla extract. Some variations may also include sour cream or heavy cream to enhance the creaminess.

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Heavy Cream
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Butter
  • Blueberries

Step by Step

  1. Prepare the crust
  2. Preheat the oven
  3. Make the filling
  4. Pour filling over the crust
  5. Bake the cheesecake
  6. Cool and chill
  7. Serve!
