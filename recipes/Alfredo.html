Alfredo Pasta

Description

Rich and creamy Fettuccine Alfredo is one of the easiest and most satisfying pasta dishes to make at home. Fresh cream is combined with parmesan cheese and butter to make a luscious Alfredo sauce in only minutes. It is then tossed with your favorite past for a delicious and hearty vegetarian Italian dinner.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine Pasta
  • Butter
  • Heeavy Cream
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Parsley

Step by Step

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Prepare the sauce
  3. Add the cream
  4. Add Cheese
  5. Combine Pasta and Sauce
  6. Serve!
